Sonohyan-Utaki Stone Gate
The gate had been built in 1519 and located in the same place with Syurijyo Castle. The gate is made from limestone and using the wooden architecture. The design of this roof is mixed with Japan and China architecture.
Behind of the gate is the area of praying for King’s good luck of his travel. Also, the gate had the role of queen’s ceremony to become priest. Now a day some elder women comes here to pray.

Tamaudun Mausoleum
The imperial tomb is called Tamaudun and built in 1501 where is located nearby Syurijyo castle. The site, covering an area of 2,442㎡ consists of two stone-walled enclosures, the three compartments of the mausoluem itself facing north and backed by a natural cliff to the south. In this tomb where stores king’s ash and his wife ash from the end of 15 centuries through 19 centuries. During the 2nd World War the tomb had been almost destroyed, but in 1977 it had restored.

Shikinaen Royal Garden
The Royal Garden had been built in 1799 for the royal family as the villa and also entertains envoys from China. The layout of the garden is that locating pond in the center of the garden, house is built by using royal architecture, and artificial mountain, fruit farm and trees are surrounding the pond. The garden was designed as Japanese style and influenced by Chinese style. This garden was designed so that ocean cannot be viewed from anywhere in the garden. One of the reasons is that the designers of the garden wanted the visitors to regard that the island is actually large.

Sefa-utaki
The spot is for the highest sanctuary area where had been created by the god “Amamikiyo” who is also known as to originate the Ryukyu Kingdam. Passing through the huge two rocks there is a view of the God Island “Kudaka Island”. This sanctuary area was prohibited to enter by the men. The highest position of the woman priest held a ceremony which had an important role of centralization for the right of kings which energized citizen’s devotion and their mental.

Katsurenjyo Castle Ruin
The castle had been built in about 1200, and became at the height of its prosperity when the military commander “Amawari” governed the castle. The traded goods with mainland Japan, Korea and China had been unearthed from the site. “Amawari” had strengthened the military force by trading business with neighboring countries and defeated the “Nakagusuku” castle military commander “Gosamaru” in 1458. After the battle, he tried to capture a stronghold of Syurijyo castle but, he had encountered by them and died.

Nakijinjyo castle Ruin
The castle was for the northern king which was made from ancient limestone and built on the hill which has about 330 feet height. One mile of the castle wall which is spreading on the curvy land looks beautiful. They had operated the trading business with Japan, China and Southeast Asia for strengthening their force. However they had been defeated by the military commander “Shohashi” from central of Ryukyu in 1416. After the battle the site had role of observation about Northern areas government by the Shurijyo castle loyal family.
