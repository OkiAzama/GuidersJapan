    address: 114 Kuwae Chatan town
    TEL: 098-936-2811
    business hours: AM9:00-PM6:00(close 1st-3rd Jan.)
    payment: Japanese yen and credit card
    homemade products: loquat jam

    Simple way to cut the loquat
    First, cut into verticle. The seeds of the loquat are located in the core, so please avoid to cut it.
    Second, scoop the seeds and peel the fruit.The white peel which is around the seeds has bitter taste, so scoop it by the spoon.
